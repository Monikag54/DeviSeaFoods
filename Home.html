About Devi Seafoods

Preferred by shrimp buyers across the world, Devi Sea Foods is located on the East coast of India - the heartland of shrimp farming. Devi is the leading exporter of shrimp from India, having vertically integrated infrastructure with own shrimp Processing, Farms, Hatchery, Feed Mills and Direct Distribution to customers.

4 Star Rated BAP certified producer

BRC Global Food Safety Certification

ASC certified Farm Produce (ASC-C-01137)

International Featured Standards Certification (IFS)

Our products

We understand needs of diverse markets and are passionate about serving them with range of Raw, Cooked and Value-added shrimp offerings.

Shrimp

The Shrimp Devi Seafoods produces a range of basic and value-added shrimp products using White Shrimp , as well as the Indian Black Tiger Shrimp. These include pre-cleaned, deveined.

Shrimp Feed

The Shrimp feed DEVEE shrimp feed is scientifically formulated to meet the complete nutritional requirement of shrimp with a balanced Amino acid profile and duly fortified with rich vitamins and minerals.

Supply Solutions

Global Sourcing- Local Solutions

Sourcing shrimp from across the Globe and providing supply solutions to Foodservice and Retail locally. Providing a solution to manage Inventory and Distribution requirements.

Sourcing Strength

Serving diverse customer preferences, with support of producers at source. Sourcing from major shrimp farming origins, directly from the producers.

Support linked to Source

Sustainability

Sustainability of the source, compliance to Social, Ethical Standards and complete Farm-to Fork traceability are key aspects of our operations we are committed to.
