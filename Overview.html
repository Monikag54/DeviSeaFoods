FEED DIVISION

Devi Seafoods launched its manufacturing operations in 2016 and introduced premium Vannamei shrimp feed to cater to growing demand for high quality feed in India, giving better control over its Supply Chain.

DEVEE brand Vannamei shrimp feed has been well received by farmers and has added to farmers trust Devi Sea Foods enjoys as a major processor. Starting with 2 production lines in 2016, we now have 8 lines operational at two manufacturing locations. This is evidence to the growth in market share, acceptance by the farmers and the feed proving its quality and performance.
